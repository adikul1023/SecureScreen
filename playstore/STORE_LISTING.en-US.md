# Play Store Listing (en-US)

## App name

SecureScreen

## Short description (80 chars max)

Protect selected apps from screenshots and screen recording

## Full description

SecureScreen helps protect sensitive app content from screenshots and screen recording.

Choose exactly which apps to protect, or enable system-wide mode when you want stronger coverage.

Key features:
- App-wise protection mode
- System-wide protection mode
- Foreground protection service with quick notification toggle
- Optional watermark overlay
- Auto-start on reboot option

How it works:
- You choose the apps you want to protect
- SecureScreen monitors the active app on your device
- Protection is applied when selected apps are in use

Permissions used:
- Usage Access: Detects the active app for protection
- Notification permission: Shows foreground service status and quick actions
- Overlay permission (optional): Used only for overlay-based features

Privacy:
- No personal data collection
- No data selling
- Settings are stored locally on device
