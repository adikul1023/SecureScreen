SecureScreen Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

Overview

SecureScreen helps prevent screenshots and screen recording for selected apps by applying Android secure window controls.

Data Collection

SecureScreen does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. Protected app selections and settings are stored locally on your device only.

Permissions Used

Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS): Used to detect the current foreground app on your device.

Foreground Service: Used to run continuous protection with a persistent notification.

Overlay Permission (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW, optional): Used only if you enable watermark overlay.

Third-Party Sharing

SecureScreen does not share data with third parties.

Contact

For support, contact your app publisher email listed on the app store page.
