SecureScreen Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

Overview

SecureScreen helps protect sensitive app content from screenshots and screen recording by applying Android secure window controls.

Data Collection

SecureScreen does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. Protected app selections and settings are stored locally on your device only.

Permissions Used

Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS): Used to detect the current foreground app on your device.

Foreground Service: Used to run continuous protection with a persistent notification.

Overlay Permission (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW, optional): Used only if you enable overlay-based features such as watermark protection.

Notification Permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Used to show the foreground service notification and quick actions.

Third-Party Sharing

SecureScreen does not share data with third parties.

Contact

For support, use the contact details listed on the app store page.
